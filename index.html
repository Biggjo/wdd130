Nigeria

Nigeria, often called the “Giant of Africa,” is a country in West Africa with a huge population and rich cultural diversity. Home to over 250 ethnic groups and hundreds of languages, it’s a place full of history, tradition, and creativity. Beyond its natural resources like oil and gas, Nigeria is also known for its lively cities such as Lagos and Abuja, its booming film industry Nollywood, and its globally celebrated Afrobeats music. It’s a country of energy and resilience, with people who are shaping culture both within Africa and around the world.
